About Us

Welcome to Raw Minds on Men's Mental Health, where we dive deep into the raw and unfiltered realities of men's mental health. Based in Vancouver, BC, our podcast is dedicated to breaking down the barriers and stigma surrounding mental health issues faced by men worldwide. Hosted by Erick, Joey, and Anthony, our show brings a fresh, honest perspective on topics that are often left unspoken. From the struggles of depression and anxiety to the complexities of relationships and personal growth, we delve into the heart of what it means to be a man in today's world. Our approach is simple yet profound—embracing vulnerability and sharing real-life stories. Erick's personal experiences, combined with the insights of our diverse guests, create a powerful platform for open dialogue. Each episode is a journey into the human experience, offering support, encouragement, and a sense of solidarity to our listeners. With a global reach spanning countries like Japan, Australia, New Zealand, and beyond, our mission is to foster understanding and promote mental wellness across cultures. Our tagline, "Breaking Down the Walls of Men's Mental Health," encapsulates our commitment to making a difference. Join us as we challenge conventions, spark conversations, and create a community where every voice is heard. At Raw Minds, we're not just talking about mental health—we're living it, breathing it, and transforming it, one story at a time. Subscribe today and be part of a movement that redefines what it means to be mentally strong. Together, let's build a future where every man can thrive.
