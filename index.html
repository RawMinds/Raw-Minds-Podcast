About Us

Welcome to Raw Minds on Men's Mental Health, where we dive deep into the raw and unfiltered realities of men's mental health. Based in Vancouver, BC, our podcast is dedicated to breaking down the barriers and stigma surrounding mental health issues faced by men worldwide. Hosted by Erick, Joey, and Anthony, our show brings a fresh, honest perspective on topics that are often left unspoken. From the struggles of depression and anxiety to the complexities of relationships and personal growth, we delve into the heart of what it means to be a man in today's world. Our approach is simple yet profound—embracing vulnerability and sharing real-life stories. Erick's personal experiences, combined with the insights of our diverse guests, create a powerful platform for open dialogue. Each episode is a journey into the human experience, offering support, encouragement, and a sense of solidarity to our listeners. With a global reach spanning countries like Japan, Australia, New Zealand, and beyond, our mission is to foster understanding and promote mental wellness across cultures. Our tagline, "Breaking Down the Walls of Men's Mental Health," encapsulates our commitment to making a difference. Join us as we challenge conventions, spark conversations, and create a community where every voice is heard. At Raw Minds, we're not just talking about mental health—we're living it, breathing it, and transforming it, one story at a time. Subscribe today and be part of a movement that redefines what it means to be mentally strong. Together, let's build a future where every man can thrive.

Meet the Hosts

Erick

Erick is a passionate advocate for men's mental health and the co-founder of Raw Minds on Men's Mental Health, a podcast that takes an honest, unfiltered approach to discussing the real struggles men face. Based in Vancouver, BC, Erick brings a deeply personal perspective to his work. After overcoming significant challenges in his life, including a difficult past in his youth, Erick is now committedto using his experiences to help others. Erick’s perspective on life and death has been shaped by the many losses he’s endured, including the deaths of his best friend and fiancée. These experiences have given him a unique outlook on death, which he shares through the open and heartfelt conversations on Raw Minds. While he has worked through the impacts of PTSD, his journey continues to influence his approach to mental health advocacy. Through Raw Minds, Erick creates a space for men to speak openly about issues such as depression, anxiety, and relationships. His raw, honest discussions encourage others to seek support and embrace vulnerability, helping to break down the stigmas surrounding mental health. Erick's passion for this work drives him, and he feels that Raw Minds is the purpose he was always meant to pursue. Outside of the podcast, Erick is a dedicated father, outdoor enthusiast, and proud dog dad to his American pocket bully, Kova.

Joey

Joey is a co-host of Raw Minds Podcast, contributing his insights on emotional well-being and personal growth.

Anthony

Anthony is the third co-host, known for his perspective on men's mental health and how it affects daily life.
